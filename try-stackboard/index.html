What is Stackboard? The Smarter Way to Organize Your Digital World Stackboard (also referred to as stack board) is an innovative productivity platform designed to help you organize, access, and manage your digital tools, links, resources, and workflows—all in one unified space.

Whether you're working on a marketing campaign, managing multiple tech stacks, creating content, or running a business, Stackboard gives you the flexibility and control to keep everything structured and at your fingertips. Why Choose Stackboard?

✅
Organize with Stacks Create customizable stacks for different areas of your work—like social media, development tools, or video creation. Each stack board can hold multiple cards, allowing you to structure your workspace in a way that mirrors your real workflow.

✅ Add Smart Cards Each card represents a tool, platform, or category—like GitHub, ChatGPT, YouTube, LinkedIn, or even custom folders. Add links, notes, shortcuts, and widgets to each card and manage them with ease.

✅ Link Control & Custom Windows Choose how links open—new tab, new window, or small overlay window—and control behavior like auto-closing, refocusing, and more. It’s perfect for split-screen tasks, quick actions, or deep research sessions.

✅ Instant Navigation with Shortcuts Access any card, board, or link using fully customizable keyboard shortcuts. With Stackboard, your entire workspace is just a few keystrokes away.

✅ Stay Organized with Drag & Drop Rearrange links, cards, and even widgets with a smooth drag-and-drop interface. Keep your stack boards clean, focused, and tailored to your workflow.

✅ Go Beyond Bookmarks Unlike traditional bookmarking tools, Stackboard helps you manage hundreds of resources with ease—ideal for developers, marketers, startups, freelancers, and digital teams.
