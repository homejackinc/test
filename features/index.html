What is Stackboard? Stackboard is a modern productivity platform that helps individuals and teams organize their tools, links, and workflows using a unique stack-and-card system. Unlike traditional bookmarking tools or task managers, Stackboard is built for how people work today—across dozens of tools, tabs, and apps. It gives users a fast, structured way to manage everything they use online, all in one place. 🧱 How Stackboard Works At the core of Stackboard is its stack and card architecture: Stacks represent categories or projects (e.g., “Marketing”, “Video Editing”, “AI Tools”). Cards inside each stack represent specific platforms or tools (like YouTube, GitHub, ChatGPT, Canva, etc.). Buttons inside each card are links to resources, dashboards, documents, or anything else—fully customizable and shortcut-enabled. This structure makes it easy to build out full workflows across multiple tools—without the clutter of bookmarks, tabs, or switching between apps. 🚀 Stackboard Features Here’s what makes Stackboard different from other productivity platforms: 🔗 Link-Centric Organization – Built specifically for organizing tools and online resources 🗂 Stacks & Cards System – Group tools and workflows with simple drag-and-drop 🪟 Smart Window Controls – Auto-resize, refocus, or close windows for a seamless experience ⌨️ Custom Shortcuts – Open tools or launch workflows with global hotkeys 📌 Pinned Buttons & Sidebar Navigation – Instant access to key links from any stack 🧠 AI Suggestions & Alerts – Get nudges like “Time to post to Facebook” or generate content with AI 🧰 Micro Apps – Built-in tools like time tracking, code editors, news feeds, crypto tickers, and more ✅ Project Manager – Organize tasks and subtasks with attached links and resources 🔌 App Integrations – Connect to Facebook, Google My Business, YouTube, and more 💡 Why Use Stackboard? If you're managing multiple tools, switching between platforms, or drowning in bookmarks and open tabs—Stackboard offers a better way.
